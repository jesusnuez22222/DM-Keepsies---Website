Meet the owner

Hi, I'm May Ann 👋

I'm May Ann Acorda, a 24-year-old entrepreneur from Davao, Philippines, and the proud founder of DM Keepsies. What started as a passion for creating meaningful, handcrafted gifts has grown into a business I pour my whole heart into every single day.

I believe that every celebration, no matter how big or small, deserves a beautiful, personal touch. Each keepsake I create is more than just a souvenir; it's a lasting reminder of the moments and people that matter most.

From weddings to baptisms, birthdays to anniversaries, I'm here to make sure your guests leave with something truly special.
